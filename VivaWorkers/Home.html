A little about us

Vivaworkers.org is a website that provides articles, commentaries, and research reports on labor, politics, and economics in Namibia and Southern Africa. Its purpose is to share information with anyone interested in labor, development, and social justice. The site's creator, Herbert Jauch, has been involved in the labor movement in Southern Africa for 30 years and has served in various roles, including as a labor researcher and director of the Labor Resource and Research Institute. He now works as a freelance labor researcher and educator with various organizations in Southern Africa and can be reached at vivaworkers@gmail.com.
